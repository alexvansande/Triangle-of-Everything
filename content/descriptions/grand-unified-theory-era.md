The epoch from $\sim 10^{-43}$ s to $\sim 10^{-36}$ s after the [[Big Bang]], when gravity has separated out but the strong, weak, and electromagnetic forces remain unified in a single grand unified interaction at energies above $\sim 10^{15}$ GeV. Grand Unified Theories (GUTs) predict this symmetry and also predict phenomena like proton decay and the existence of magnetic monopoles — neither yet observed. The GUT era ends when the strong nuclear force breaks away from the electroweak force in a phase transition that releases enormous energy, likely triggering the exponential expansion known as [[Inflation Era|INFLATION]]. This symmetry breaking may also explain the matter-antimatter asymmetry observed in the universe today.
