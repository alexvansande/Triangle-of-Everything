A milliliter of pure water — one cubic centimeter, one gram. Water's density of exactly 1 g/cm³ originally defined the gram itself, making it the universal reference for mass and volume. On this chart, a single drop sits near the center of the human-scale world, between a [[Grain of Sand]] and a [[Nickel|coin]].
