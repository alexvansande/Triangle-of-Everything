Our home galaxy — a barred spiral containing 100–400 billion stars, including our [[Sun]], and spanning about 100,000 light-years. The Solar System orbits roughly 26,000 light-years from [[Sgr A*]], the supermassive black hole at the galactic center. On a collision course with [[Andromeda (M31)|Andromeda]], due to merge in about 4.5 billion years.
